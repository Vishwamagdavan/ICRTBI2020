General Instructions

  • Authors are required to submit their papers (maximum of 8 A4 sized pages) adhering to the specified format (a copy of which is available for download in the site).
  • Authors must upload their papers onto the conference website.(* Don't send your paper in the email.)
  • The submitted papers must be original and unpublished.
  • Double blind peer reviews will be followed.
  • Registrations must be done online through our website.
