About the college

St.Joseph’s Institute of Technology, a St.Joseph’s group of Institutions started in the year 2011.It is a Christian Minority Institution with six streams viz CSE, EEE, ECE, IT , MECH & CIVIL. The college has best infrastructure spread across 4 lakhs sq.ft with well equipped laboratories, workshops and classrooms.
A state of the art infrastructure including auditorium, mess halls, administrative block covering an area of 10 lakhs sq.ft. is upcoming. Our college as one of the leading engineering colleges, focuses on inculcating not only knowledge but also good ethics to the budding engineers of future India. This proves to be the uniqueness of St. Joseph’s Institute of Technology.
